About Ellista International

Founded in 2007 by Mr. Mohamed Fiaz Khazi. Ellista International is a systems solution provider in the Doors and windows, Architectural systems, Outdoor shading, Interior shades, Acoustic solutions, and aluminum and glazing industry.

With a strong diverse and multicultural team of 600 employees in 4 countries, Ellista International delivers high-quality products and services.
